March 2023

Massive Open Online Training

DEVI Sansthan's first Massive Open Online Training was launched in March. With separate sessions conducted in Hindi and English, the 3-hour online workshops were a hit, reaching over 25,000 views. The sessions were joined by numerous government and private teachers, as well as representatives from national and international organisations. With participation from 50 countries and 26 Indian states, the highly interactive and engaging training session used videos to take viewers inside ALfA classrooms. Panel discussions were interspersed with hands-on activities, live audience polls and interaction through the chat.

Disruptive Talks for Literacy

D-Talks is an online forum that brings together change-makers from around the world to share their solutions to the global literacy crisis and commit to urgent action for achieving Universal Foundational Literacy and Numeracy, as envisioned by Sustainable Development Goal 4. More than 174 speakers from 22 countries have participated in the D-Talks since its launch in 2021. The speakers and audience of the D-Talks include diverse stakeholders including top government policymakers, heads of CSR foundations, international NGOs and foundations and educators across India and globally.

Do you have a great story to tell about educational transformation? Is your organisation creating innovative, scalable literacy solutions? Come be a guest on D-Talks

Publications & Media

Discover a transformative approach to teaching and learning with 'Accelerating Learning for All,' a comprehensive guide for teachers and a practical roadmap for policymakers. Brimming with anecdotes from teachers, principals, and students, this book inspires and equips us to build a better education system.

Disruptive Literacy

“Foundational literacy and numeracy are crucial to every person’s well-being. A nation’s literacy rate is a key predictor of its social and economic progress. Yet the world still has hundreds of millions of children in school but nor learning the basics, and adults deprived of these foundational skills. Why?

Drawing from remarkable examples of movements around the world, and sprinkled with stories from the authors’ grassroots educational work in the Global Dream Accelerating Learning for All (ALfA) program, Disruptive Literacy is an easy-to-read but hard-to-ignore manifesto that will touch your heart and inspire you to action.”

Shri Rajnath Singh, Defence Minister, India, in support of ALfA Accelerating Learning for All
