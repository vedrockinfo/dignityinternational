The ALfA Way App is a comprehensive resource tool that offers a diverse collection of global digital books in various languages. It provides login IDs for students, teachers, and surveyors, making it accessible to a broad audience.

In the wake of the Covid pandemic, we realized that in the future, much of education will be digital. To this end, we’ve developed the Literacy Now App, which contains an easy-to-use repository of videos and interactive games, to teach foundational literacy and numeracy in an enjoyable and effective manner.

The app enables partner organizations like governments, schools and NGOs to use large-scale data for decision-making. DEVI Sansthan recently signed an MOU with the Department of Secondary Education, Uttar Pradesh, in which students will use the Literacy Now App to teach their illiterate neighbours, relatives and community members.

Start teaching with LiteracyNow today!

Got the App but need some help getting started? Let us step you through it:

  • Register yourself and your learner(s).
  • Conduct a pre-test to check their existing level.
  • Start teaching.
  • Conduct a post-test.
  • Get verified and receive a certificate.

The Literacy Now app aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 4 (Quality Education) by providing accessible and engaging literacy resources to empower learners worldwide. The app enables learners to unlock their potential and contribute to a more equitable and educated world, in line with SDG 4's vision of inclusive and quality education for all.

The Pragati TARGETPlus App instantly captures data and delivers reports and results from tests or surveys with the press of a button. Reports are provided on the spot and are designed to enable immediate corrective actions by various stakeholders. This prompt feedback and corrective action are aimed at enhancing student performance, which the Pragati app effectively supports. Its versatile and useful features contribute to its effectiveness. Installing the Pragati App on Android and iOS devices is straightforward through the Play Store or App Store.
